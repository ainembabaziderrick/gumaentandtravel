    Our Story

    GUMA ENT AND TRAVEL LTD is a dynamic entrepreneurship and travel company committed to providing innovative business solutions and reliable travel services to individuals, organizations, tourists, entrepreneurs, and corporate clients. We combine business innovation with exceptional customer service to create opportunities, experiences, and long-term partnerships across Uganda, East Africa, and international markets.

    • Professional travel & tourism services
    • Entrepreneurship and business consultancy support
    • Corporate travel management and event coordination

    At GUMA ENT AND TRAVEL LTD, we believe that travel opens doors to opportunity while entrepreneurship builds sustainable growth. Our services are designed to connect people, businesses, and destinations efficiently and professionally.

    Our Mission

    “To provide reliable, innovative, and affordable travel and business solutions that empower people, connect opportunities, and create meaningful experiences across Uganda and beyond.”

    Our Vision

    “To become East Africa’s leading entrepreneurship and travel company recognized for excellence, integrity, innovation, and transformative customer experiences.”
